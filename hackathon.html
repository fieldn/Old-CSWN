Microsoft Hackathon Event!
Come One, Come All!

On November 22, 2014, CSWN will be hosting in association with Microsoft a 12-hour hackathon. Students of all disciplines are encouraged and welcomed to come!

Starting at 7:30am, the event will run until approximately 9:30 pm.

Don't miss out on the opportunity to brainstorm with other kids and create something you can call your own!

Mentors will be available to all students to help in the process, and at the end of the day a panel of judges will evaluate what you've created. Prizes are/include (?)
