BS/MS and Grad School: Differing Paths Beyond Undergrads
Oct 27th Ever thought of going beyond an undergraduate degree, but not sure what you can do or where to start? Do you wonder about getting a Masters or PhD? Here at Purdue, there are different ways to further your education. CSWN is proud to present to you the opportunity to meet with three of our fellow CSWN ladies, Alicia Klinvex, Lauren Stuart and Emily Friesen. Alicia is a doctoral student in her fourth year and is pursuing her PhD. Lauren graduated from Purdue last May and is currently attending graduate school in Purdue. Emily is a senior who participated in the BS/MS program. These awesome ladies will share their experiences with you and would gladly answer any questions you may have about these paths of furthering your education. Food and drinks will be provided! :)
